- storpunk.io: HTTPS required and configured

## DNS Configuration Required
For redirect domains (storepunk.com, storpunk.com, storpunk.net, storpunk.org):
1. A record pointing to same IP as storpunk.io
2. Server-level 301 redirects configured
3. SSL certificates installed for HTTPS redirects

- Alternative spellings secured (storepunk.com)
